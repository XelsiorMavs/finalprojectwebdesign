✨ Navigation Bar Component

This is a standalone, reusable navbar built with red, white & black theme colors. Copy the <header> block above and paste it into your shop.html, about.html, and profile.html files.

📌 How to use:
1. Copy the entire <header>...</header> block.
2. Paste it right after the opening <body> tag in your other files.
3. Update the href attributes if your file names differ.
4. Change the class="active" to the current page's link.
