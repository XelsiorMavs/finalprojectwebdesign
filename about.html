Modern Philippine Staples

Easy denim, relaxed layers, and wardrobe pieces made for everyday life in the Philippines.

Jag Jeans is designed to offer high-quality and easy-to-wear wardrobe staples under a modern lens that reflects the relaxed and laidback mood of a coastal Philippines lifestyle.

Our collections are versatile, buildable, and interchangeable, aiming to provide a transeasonal capsule wardrobe designed for a wide range of lifestyles.

We live by work-to-weekend styling, offering better basics, denim, and layering staples that can be worn in school, at work, on casual days, or during weekend plans around the Philippines.

We focus on responsible design and production, using natural fibres wherever possible and creating quality pieces that are made to last. Read more about our responsibility journey.
